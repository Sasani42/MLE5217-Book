# Week 2 — What to expect

Next lecture is about **describing data** — the everyday summary numbers we reach
for *before* any machine learning happens.

**You will meet these ideas:**

- *Descriptive and Inferential Statistics*
- *Mean, median, mode* — three ways to say “a typical value”.
- *Variance* and *standard deviation* — how spread out the values are.
- *Distributions*, focusing on the **normal** (“bell”) shape.
- *Sampling* — how the data we happen to have relates to the data we don't.

**Words to listen for** (we will use these again in later weeks, so they should
start to feel familiar):

- **line of best fit** — just a first glimpse this week; we formalise it in Week 3.
- **correlation** - the degree of agreement between two variables
- **multicollinearity** — when two inputs move together; it comes back in Week 7.
