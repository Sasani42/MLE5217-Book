Watching the residuals shrink

A line sweeps through the Vickers-hardness data. The dashed vertical lines mark each residual - the gap between a measured point and the line. As the line drifts away from the data, the residuals stretch; as it settles into the best fit, they collapse. The number that the fit minimises is the sum of squared residuals, shown at right.
